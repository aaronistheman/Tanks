About the Author

These webpages were designed and programmed by me. I made them to gain some experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. In this process, I also gained experience with the jQuery library and with jQuery AJAX. Furthermore, I looked into languages such as PHP and Bootstrap, although I did not use them in these webpages. Of course, I could not have done it without W3Schools, whose tutorials I used to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

The game Tanks was also programmed by me. I made it to gain a greater grasp of object-oriented programming, C++, and the SFML library. The book SFML Game Development by Jan Haller, Henrik Vogelius Hansson, and Artur Moreira helped me greatly by providing me with a way to approach game development.

For what it is worth, my name is Aaron Singh.
